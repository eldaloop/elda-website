Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Elda Loop LLC ("we," "our," or "us") handles your information when you use the Elda Loop app. We believe in being straightforward about data practices.

What We Collect

Account Information: When you sign up, we collect your email, name, and age range. You can optionally add profile details like photos and interests.

Location Data: We use your zip code for local recommendations. When using Journey Mode (navigation), we access precise location with your permission. You can disable this anytime in your device settings.

Preferences: Your cuisine preferences, budget, dining companions, and activity interests — all so we can give you better recommendations.

Usage Data: How you interact with the app — plans viewed, places visited, items saved to your Vault.

Messages: If you use our chat feature, we store messages to provide the service.

How We Use It

  • Provide personalized recommendations
  • Enable navigation and Journey Mode
  • Facilitate connections with other users
  • Improve our AI recommendation engine
  • Send service communications (verification, password resets)
  • Send promotional content (only with consent)
  • Analyze usage to improve the app
  • Prevent fraud and abuse

Third-Party Services

  • Supabase — Authentication and database
  • Google Places API — Venue search and information
  • Mapbox — Maps and navigation
  • Anthropic Claude API — AI-powered recommendations

Data Sharing

We don't sell your personal information. We may share data in these cases:

  • With Other Users: If you enable social features, your public profile and open activities are visible to others
  • Service Providers: Third-party services listed above, as needed to operate the app
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect rights and safety
  • Business Transfers: In case of merger or acquisition (we'd notify you)

Data Retention

We keep your data as long as your account is active. Delete your account anytime in settings — we'll remove your data within 30 days. Some anonymized usage data may be retained for analytics.

Your Rights

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and data
  • Export your data
  • Opt out of promotional emails
  • Disable location tracking

Email privacy@eldaloop.com to exercise these rights.

Security

We use encryption in transit and at rest, secure authentication, and regular security reviews. No system is 100% secure, but we take this seriously.

Age Requirement

Elda Loop is for users 18 and older. We don't knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe someone under 18 has signed up, contact us.

California Residents

CCPA provides additional rights: right to know what data we collect, right to delete, right to opt out of sales (we don't sell data anyway).

Changes

We may update this policy. We'll notify you of significant changes via the app or email.
